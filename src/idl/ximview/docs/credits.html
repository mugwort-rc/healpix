Credits

Ximview has been significantly influenced by Google Maps, SAO Image DS9, Aaron Barth's ATV (especially the Asinh scaling), and NRAO's AIPS (for Imstats and Peakfit). It makes essential use of the HEALPix IDL library, and NASA's IDL Astrolib, especially the World Coordinates System library and several FITS I/O packages. I would like to thank Tess Jaffe, Sam Leitch, Tony Banday, Stuart Lowe and Eric Hivon for testing, bug reports, and suggestions. Thanks also to David Fanning for his invaluable website on IDL.
